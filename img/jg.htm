    “It's just super interesting because the question that I think is the most valuable in terms of anything, really, is why. The why. Why do you want to lose weight? Why do you want to be eating greens every day? Why do you want the job? Why do you need the relationship? It's the why that we don’t pay enough attention to. What is the actual thing you're going after and your reason for it? ...
    Because so much of the ego gets in the way - ‘I think I need this’ - again, for self validation or validation from others. Life becomes about this perception of ourselves instead of what our values are. Are we living in line with our values and what feels genuine to our gut? I think it’s important to check in on that.”
